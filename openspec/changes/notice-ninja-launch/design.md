## Context
The app should feel calm, practical, and trustworthy — more like a renter's operations desk than a generic chatbot.

## Decisions
- Use a single-page dashboard with source text intake on the left and structured action output on the right.
- Make AI central to extracting obligations, deadlines, uncertainty, and response options.
- Emphasize clarity, caveats, and next actions rather than pretending to give legal advice.
- Optimize for "paste the notice -> understand obligations -> send the reply".
