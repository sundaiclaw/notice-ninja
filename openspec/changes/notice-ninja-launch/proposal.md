## Why
Renters often receive lease clauses, renewal terms, or landlord messages that are stressful and unclear, especially around notice timing, move-out obligations, and deposits.

## What Changes
- Build an AI app that interprets housing/lease notice text and the renter's situation.
- Generate likely deadlines, missing-information flags, move-out or renewal checklist items, and a reply draft.
- Help users respond faster and more confidently without manually decoding legal-ish language.
